The Flight

 

You find yourself sitting at a window seat on a plane going to Hawaii for your summer vacation. You look outside the window and you see the left wing of the plane and the sunset over the cloud. You turn away from the window and look inside the plane.

You look around and see the plane is relatively empty. You also notice the emergency door next to your seat. At the corner of your eye, you notice the flight attendant walking towards you with a cart of drinks. The flight attendant asks if you would like anything to drink.
